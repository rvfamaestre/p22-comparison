\subsection{Our contributions}
	\begin{itemize}
    \item Modeling mixed traffic with human-driven(HDVs) and connected autonomous vehicles (CAVs), improving capacity and stability, in addition to extending traffic analysis from open platoons to closed ring-road systems.
    \item Proposing a mesoscopic RL framework combining micro- and macro-level traffic states with incorporated resistance forces on our input to create the nearest real life simulation. 
\end{itemize}
